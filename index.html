About Me

I am a senior student majoring in the Department of Information Management and minoring in the Department of Economics at National Taiwan University, NTU. My research interests span economics of social interactions, information economics, structural and empirical marketing science and online learning behavior. Currently, I am fortunate to work with Prof. Chih-Sheng Hsieh on social interactions and applied econometrics.

Previously, I was working with Prof. Lin-Shan Lee on web development.
